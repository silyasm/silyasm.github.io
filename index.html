About Us

[Organization Name] is dedicated to supporting young muslim students who want to pursue medicine by providing opportunities and resources, aiming to help sanctify their resolve and fortify their path. We serve undergraduate students, medical students, residents, and doctors by incorporating the brotherhood of Islam striving to create a community as well as educate others on the Islamic path of medicine.

Our mission is to:

  • Increase discourse between muslim students and physicians at all levels of the academic and professional ladder.
  • Empower Muslim students to navigate the challenges of medical school and beyond.
  • Support the understanding of islamic jurisprudence in medicine.

Resources

Mentorship Program

Our mentorship program follows a structured hierarchy, connecting undergraduates with medical students, medical students with residents, and residents with doctors. While priority is given to connections within this hierarchy, participants are encouraged to explore mentorship opportunities outside of it if they find a suitable match. This flexible approach ensures that individuals receive tailored guidance while allowing for connections that best serve their needs and aspirations.

Panels

Attend informative panels featuring Muslim doctors and healthcare professionals discussing various medical fields and career paths.

Fiqh Talks

Our initiative seeks to bridge the realms of Islam and medicine, intertwining the ethical principles of Islamic jurisprudence (fiqh) with the advancements and challenges of modern medical practice.
